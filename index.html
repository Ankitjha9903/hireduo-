    About Us?

    Empowering Your Business with Tailored HR Solutions

    • HireDuo offers a wide range of HR solutions tailored to meet the diverse needs of its clients. This includes contract and general staffing services, IT recruitment, and managed services. Their broad service offering ensures that they can address various hiring and staffing challenges effectively, whether it’s finding temporary staff, sourcing IT talent, or managing overall recruitment processes.

    • Established in 2015 and with ten years of experience, HireDuo has built a reputation for excellence in the HR industry. Their team of highly qualified professionals brings extensive knowledge and expertise to the table, which helps them provide top-notch recruitment and staffing solutions. This wealth of experience is instrumental in understanding client needs and delivering tailored solutions that enhance client satisfaction.

    • Headquartered in Kolkata, HireDuo has expanded its reach to serve over 100 clients across India and internationally. This broad client base highlights their capability to manage and support a diverse range of organizations, reflecting their adaptability and effectiveness in various market conditions. Their commitment to client satisfaction is evident through their extensive network and successful track record.
